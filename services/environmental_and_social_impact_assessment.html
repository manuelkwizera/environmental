Environmental and social Impact assessment

Genius Environmental, a registered and leading firm in Rwanda, exemplifies excellence in the realm of environmental conservation and sustainable development. Our collaborations extend to both local and international entities, ensuring developmental initiatives harmonize with environmental and social welfare standards. With a team steeped in expertise and a commitment that transcends basic compliance, Genius Environmental pioneers in balancing developmental aspirations with ecological sustainability and community well-being.
