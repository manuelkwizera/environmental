Comprehensive Air Pollution and Noise Monitoring, Impact Quantification, and Pollution Compliance Assessment at SteeRwa industries

This project mainly focused on Continuous Chimney emissions and ambient air pollution monitoring, quantify and assess the impact of SteelRwa Insustries on the neighborhood air pollution and workers exposure level at this factor focusing on the following critical air pollutants.

  • Particulate matter with an aerodynamic diameter less than 2.5µm (PM2.5)
  • Particulate matter with an aerodynamic diameter less than 10 µm (PM10)
  • Carbon monoxide (CO)
  • Sulfur Dioxide (SO2)
  • Oxide of nitrogen (NO2)
  • Ground ozone (O3)
    •

    Compliance Assessment We assess the measured air and noise pollutant levels against stringent local air quality standards.

    Through this project, we provide valuable insights into the prevailing air quality conditions, identify the sources of pollution, and offer essential data for compliance management. Our commitment to environmental preservation and sustainability drives our endeavor to help Century Engineering Contractors Ltd make informed decisions for a cleaner and healthier working environment.

    From: March 2019 To: Present

    Location: Rwamagana District, Eastern Province of Rwanda
