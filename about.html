About Us

Genius Environmental Ltd., formerly Estonteco Brila Ltd., is a pioneering private company that has firmly established itself within the environmental sector in Rwanda. With a strong commitment to sustainable practices and environmental stewardship, we have earned recognition as a Lead Expert Firm by the Rwanda Association of Environmental Practitioners (RAPEP), holding the prestigious registration number RAPEP/EA/229.

At Genius Environmental Ltd., innovation is at the core of our ethos. We constantly seek new and groundbreaking approaches to tackle environmental challenges. Our projects are not just about compliance; they are about driving meaningful change. We take pride in our ability to deliver cost-effective solutions that not only meet regulatory requirements but also enhance the overall quality of life for communities and ecosystems. Our work leaves a lasting positive impact, creating a legacy of environmental sustainability for the regions we serve.
